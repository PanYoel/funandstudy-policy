Hero Hunmin: Account Deletion Policy

This page provides information on the account deletion process and related data handling for the Hero Hunmin app, developed by Fun And Study (Pan-Yeol Park). We respect your right to control your data and provide a transparent and simple account deletion procedure.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account directly from within the Hero Hunmin app.

  1. Launch and Log In: Open the Hero Hunmin app on your device and log in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Navigate to Options: Go to the Options menu from the game's main screen.
  3. Select Delete Account: Tap the Delete Account button.
  4. Final Confirmation: A warning message will appear, stating Deleting your account will permanently remove all cloud-saved game data and cannot be undone. Please read it carefully. Proceeding with the final confirmation will process the account deletion immediately.

Data Deletion and Retention

Upon your request for account deletion, your personal information and game save files will be deleted. Please see the details below.

Data Deleted Immediately:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account information (personal information such as email address, UID, login credentials).
  • All your game progress data saved to the cloud (game save files).

Data Deleted Within 1 Week:

  • Your personal ranking information in the weekly ranking system: To ensure the stability of the weekly ranking aggregation, your rank data may take up to 7 days to be completely removed from the leaderboard after your deletion request.

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Fully anonymized statistical data that cannot identify an individual (for example, overall app usage collected via Firebase Analytics) may be retained for service improvement and statistical analysis. This data is not linked to any specific account.
